What is Nebrix?

Nebrix is an active development, community focused platform where anyone can create, share, and play games with friends! Our goal is to give developers the tools they need to bring their ideas to life, and share their games to the world, all in one platform for free!

With Nebrix Studio you can build any game imaginable, with the only limit being your imagination. With Nebrix Player, you can explore a wide variety of community made games and enjoy endless fun with your friends in multiplayer!

Nebrix is built around creativity, accessibility, and most importantly, the community, giving everyone the opportunity to turn their ideas into reality!
