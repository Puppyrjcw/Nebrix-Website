## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

Before using Nebrix Studio or playing games, you'll need to create free Nebrix account.

1. Visit **[nebrixgames.com](https://www.nebrixgames.com)**
2. Fill in the **Create Account** form on the homepage:
   - Choose a username (3–20 characters, letters/numbers/underscores/hyphens)
   - Enter your email address
   - Enter your birthday
   - Choose a password
   - Confirm your password
3. Click **Sign Up**
4. Download Nebrix Client to play Nebrix games!

***

### Installing Nebrix Studio

Nebrix Studio is the creation tool for building and publishing games.

1. Download the latest **`NebrixStudioSetup.exe`** from the [Releases page](https://github.com/Puppyrjcw/Nebrix/releases)
2. Run the installer
3. Launch **Nebrix Studio**
4. Sign in with your Nebrix account username and password
5. Click **New Project** to create your first game!

***

### Installing Nebrix Client

Nebrix Client lets you play games published on the Nebrix platform!

1. Download the latest **`NebrixClientSetup.exe`** from the [Releases page](https://github.com/Puppyrjcw/Nebrix/releases)
2. Run the installer
3. Once installed, visit **[nebrixgames.com](https://www.nebrixgames.com)** and click **Play** on any game!
4. Sign in with your Nebrix account and the game will load automatically!

> If the Client fails to load automatically try reinstalling. If it does not succeed contact us at support@nebrixgames.com
